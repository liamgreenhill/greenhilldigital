iOS and Android app development

You have a killer idea for an app that’s going to change everything. We can bring it to life.

We enjoy nothing more than talking to creative people with great ideas. And we’re regularly approached by start-ups with an app idea – often something ingenious that fills a genuine gap in the market – to provide advice and assistance to flesh out their brief, develop specifications then provide the design and technical skills to bring that idea to life.

Apps come in different shapes and sizes but many follow a common theme. By integrating with a remote data store – usually via an API – they can:

  • register and authenticate users;
  • provide the user with instant access to a personalised experience;
  • retrieve data from and save data to the remote data store;
  • allow the execution of a small number of important tasks in as fast and user-friendly a manner as possible; and
  • draw users back into the app via Push Notifications.

In addition, apps tend to require content-management and related tools so that administrators can author and schedule the content being sent to app users.

We have the skills and experience to provide all of the elements of this package.

We engage in UX (User Experience) Design methodologies to identify then deconstruct key user goals and paths. We then design blueprint solutions, providing wireframes and other prototypes for illustration.

We’ll then provide visual designs of your app patterns and screens, introducing branding, texture, colour, tone and typography to provide the requisite spark.

We have the back-end development skills and experience to create the remote web database application/s housing your data. And we can enable secure access to that data by adding a REST API.

Lastly – we can develop and launch your app. We’re well versed in the various development technologies and testing tools required for both iOS and Android and will help you plan a MVP (minimum viable product) so as to launch as soon as possible. We’ll then follow that up with regular update releases based on our "release early, release often" philosophy.

Greenhill has designed and developed iOS and Android apps for a variety of clients including Medtronic and The University of Strathclyde. (Our portfolio is available on request.) If you have an app idea and require assistance bringing it to life, please get in touch – we’d love to hear from you.
